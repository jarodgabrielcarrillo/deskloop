Free Deep Work Tracker — No Account Required

The Deskloop Deep Work Tracker helps you measure and improve the quality of your focused work time. Log sessions with a live timer, rate your focus quality from 1–5 stars, and track your weekly deep work hours on a visual chart — all stored privately in your browser.

Deep work, a concept popularized by Cal Newport, refers to cognitively demanding tasks performed in a state of distraction-free concentration. Research consistently shows that tracking these sessions increases both the quantity and quality of focused work over time.

Your focus score is calculated from your weekly hours, session count, average quality rating, and consistency across days — giving you a single number that reflects how well your week is going. No account, no subscription, no data sent to any server.
