How Much Does Your Meeting Really Cost?

The Deskloop Meeting Cost Calculator converts your team's combined time into real money — updated every second from the moment you start the clock. It supports live currency conversion across 17 currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, PHP, INR, SGD, and more, powered by real-time exchange rates from the European Central Bank.

Studies consistently show that unnecessary meetings are one of the largest hidden costs in modern organizations. A 1-hour meeting with 8 people at an average salary of $80,000/year costs over $307 — before accounting for the time it takes to mentally re-engage with focused work afterward.

Use this tool before scheduling a recurring meeting, or run it live during a meeting to create a shared sense of urgency around staying on-agenda and finishing on time.
