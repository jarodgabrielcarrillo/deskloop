How Much Does Your Meeting Really Cost?

The Meeting Cost Calculator converts your team's time into real dollars, updated every second. It works by calculating the combined hourly rate of all attendees and running it as a live counter from the moment you start the clock.

Studies consistently show that unnecessary meetings are one of the largest drains on organizational productivity. A 1-hour meeting with 8 people at $80,000/year average salary costs over $307 — before accounting for the time it takes to mentally re-engage with deep work afterward.

Use this tool before scheduling recurring meetings, or run it live during a meeting to create a shared sense of urgency. Knowing the real cost of a meeting changes how you run them.
