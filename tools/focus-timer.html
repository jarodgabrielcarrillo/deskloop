Free Online Pomodoro Focus Timer

The Deskloop Focus Timer uses the Pomodoro Technique — a time management method that breaks work into focused intervals separated by short breaks. The default is 25 minutes of focus followed by a 5-minute break, with a longer 15-minute break every 4 sessions.

Research consistently shows that working in focused intervals improves concentration and reduces mental fatigue. The Pomodoro Technique, developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s, remains one of the most effective and widely-used productivity systems.

This timer works entirely in your browser — no download, no account, no subscription. Customize your focus and break intervals, track your daily sessions, and keep the tab open while you work.
