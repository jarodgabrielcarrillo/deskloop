Interactive Timezone Band World Map

The Deskloop Time Zone Overlap Finder renders an accurate world map with each timezone band as a distinct clickable region — similar to standard world time zone maps. Click any colored band to add it to your comparison, then instantly see which hours overlap for your entire team in the 24-hour grid below.

Country borders are rendered on top of the timezone bands for geographic reference. The timezone regions follow actual political boundaries using Natural Earth geographic data. Free, no signup, no data stored.
