Random Task Picker — Beat Decision Paralysis

The Deskloop Task Decider helps you overcome the common productivity trap of spending more time deciding what to work on than actually working. Add your pending tasks, spin the wheel, and let randomness make the call — then commit and start.

Decision paralysis is a well-documented productivity killer. When faced with multiple important tasks, the brain often defaults to inaction or easy low-value work. Removing the decision entirely — with a spin of the wheel — tricks the brain into action and bypasses overthinking.

Use this tool when your task list feels overwhelming, when every task feels equally important, or when you just need a nudge to start. Works best combined with the Focus Timer — spin first, then lock in with a Pomodoro session.
