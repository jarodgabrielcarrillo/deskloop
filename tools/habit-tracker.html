Free Online Habit Tracker — No Account Required

The Deskloop Habit Tracker lets you build and maintain daily habits directly in your browser, with no signup, no app download, and no subscription. Your habits and streaks are saved locally on your device using browser storage — nothing is sent to a server.

Research consistently shows that habit tracking dramatically improves follow-through. Seeing your streak grow creates a psychological commitment to not "break the chain" — a technique popularized by Jerry Seinfeld and widely adopted in productivity circles.

Add up to any number of habits, check them off each day, and watch your weekly consistency patterns emerge. Your data stays private on your device and persists across browser sessions.
