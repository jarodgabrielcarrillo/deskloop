Free Online Work Hours Logger — No Account Required

The Deskloop Work Hours Logger lets you track work sessions directly in your browser with zero setup. Start a session timer, tag it with a project name, and build a complete log of where your working hours go — all stored locally on your device.

Whether you're a freelancer tracking billable hours, a remote worker logging your day, or a student managing study sessions, this tool gives you a clear picture of how your time is actually spent. Export your log to CSV at any time for invoicing or reporting.

Your session data is saved privately in your browser using local storage. No account, no subscription, no data sent to any server. Works on desktop and mobile.
